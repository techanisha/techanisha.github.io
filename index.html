NIVA is a modern Nepali fashion boutique focused on affordable-luxury dresses and sets for women who want confidence without overthinking. We believe a good outfit should feel beautiful, practical, photo-ready, and still reachable.

Our motive

We serve women across Nepal who are building their own style: students, creators, young professionals, sisters, bridesmaids, and anyone who wants a clean, polished dress or set for real plans.

Our promise

Simple browsing, clear fit notes, COD, Nepal-wide delivery, honest tracking after NCM booking, and fast support through Instagram and TikTok.

Privacy

Privacy & Cookies

We collect only the details needed to prepare order requests, provide delivery updates, and support customers. We do not sell your personal information.

Data we use

Name, phone, district, full address, landmark, order items, payment method, order status, tracking number after dispatch, optional email, optional Instagram handle, and customer notes.

Cookies and local storage

This public launch uses browser storage on your device for your cart and receipt draft. Please send your NIVA order number to @niva.creation_ on Instagram so the boutique can manually confirm stock and delivery. A secure account and order backend is planned for the next release.

Your choices

Terms

Terms of Service

By ordering from NIVA, you agree that prices are listed in Nepali Rupees and delivery is handled by NCM Courier or partner couriers after order confirmation.

Orders and payment

Cash on Delivery is currently active. This public launch creates a receipt draft on your device; send the NIVA order number to @niva.creation_ so stock, price, and delivery can be manually confirmed. Requests may be cancelled if stock is unavailable, customer details are incomplete, or delivery cannot be completed.

Product accuracy

Colors and fit may vary slightly by screen, lighting, body shape, and fabric behavior. Please check size guidance before ordering.

Tracking

NCM tracking numbers are available only after we book the parcel with NCM. Before that, use your NIVA order number when messaging @niva.creation_.

Returns

Returns or exchanges are accepted only for products damaged during delivery or wrong-size/wrong-item/order issues reported within 3 days with clear photos.

Return Policy

NIVA accepts return or exchange requests only when the product is damaged during delivery, or when the delivered item/size is different from the confirmed order.

Return window

Message us within 3 days of delivery with your order number, unboxing/product photos, and a clear description of the issue.

Not eligible

Change of mind, dislike after delivery, wrong size chosen by the customer, worn/washed items, removed tags, perfume stains, makeup marks, and damage after use are not eligible.

Refund method

Approved COD refunds are processed by bank transfer or store credit after inspection. Delivery fees are refunded only if NIVA sent the wrong/damaged item.
